Pancake

Description

This is one of the best deserts that is very versitile. It can be eaten with chocolate, ice-cream, nuts, fruites and of course cream. Must be a skilled fliper though.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 3 cups of flower
  • 200 ml of milk
  • 100 ml of carbonated water
  • teaspoom of sugar
  • teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Mix eggs
  2. Add wet ingredients and continue mixing
  3. Add dry ingredients and continue mixing
  4. Add milk if batter is too thick
  5. Spread on a oiled pan and fry
  6. Flip on each side
  7. Add your prefered toppings
