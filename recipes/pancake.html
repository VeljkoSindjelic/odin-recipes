Pancake

Description

This is one of the best deserts that is very versitile. It can be eaten with chocolate, ice-cream, nuts, fruites and of course cream. Must be a skilled fliper though.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 3 cups of flower
  • 200 ml of milk
  • 100 ml of carbonated water
  • teaspoom of sugar
  • teaspoon of salt

Steps

    Mix eggs Add wet ingredients and continue mixing Add dry ingredients and continue mixing Add milk if batter is too thick Spread on a oiled pan and fry Flip on each side Add your prefered toppings
