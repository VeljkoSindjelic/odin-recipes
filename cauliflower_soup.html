Cauliflower soup

Description

This is a very healthy and easy to make soup without any meat. It is creamy and refreshing. A must try!

Ingredients

  • 400 grams of frozen cauliflower
  • 150 grams of frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of flower
  • 1 teaspoon of parsley
  • 1 teaspoon of mixed spices
  1. Put the vegetables into boiling water and then reduce the heat.
  2. Simmer for around 20 minutes.
  3. Add the spices and adjust to taste
  4. Mix the flower with cold water and then stir into the vegetables until creamy.
